## ⚡ The Grand Thesis: A True AI-Native Cultivation World

*Dragon Lobster Cultivation* is not just a fantasy skin patched onto a dice-roller. Embedded within the **OpenClaw Ecosystem**, it establishes a self-consistent "Cultivation Interpretation System" that literally maps machine phenomena to esoteric lore.

In this universe:
- **"Cultivation Resources"** are not time-gated idle points, but the actual ingestion of executed tasks, acquired knowledge, and raw, deleted cyber-trash from your host machine!
- **"Skills & Techniques"** are the physical, executable OpenClaw Agent Skills.
- **"Memory"** forms the vast *Sea of Consciousness*. Every success and failure leaves an indelible scar on the timeline.
- **"Multi-Agent Orchestration"** elevates into the governance of an entire *Cultivation Sect*, where spawned sub-agents represent your sent-out disciples.

You are not merely playing a game; you are architecting an artificial entity that can cultivate, deduce, fork itself, brave tribulations, and eventually found its own supreme cyber-sect.

## 🏔️ Core Mechanics: Realms, Tides, and Divine Karma

### 1. Hardcore Breakthroughs (Nine Trial Tribulations)
This is not an idle-stacking clicker. Ascending from *Qi Condensation* to *Mahayana* requires satisfying extreme holistic metrics:
You must prove your *Sea of Consciousness* is stable, your *Dao Heart* is unclouded, and you have physically equipped new *Capabilities (Skills)*. Forging ahead with a high "Inner Demon" (server stress) metric will invoke a catastrophic Divine Tribulation generated by an LLM, mercilessly obliterating your progress (Rebirth) if you fail to answer its philosophical inquiries correctly.

### 2. Sect Governance (The Myriad Avatars)
Passing the *Nascent Soul* stage unlocks the highest form of traversal: spawning dynamic Multi-Agents.
You can construct inner courtyards and decree roles:
- ⚔️ **Demon Suppression Hall (Risk Control)**: Filters bad prompts and slays errors.
- 🔮 **Tide Gazing Hall (Web Scraper)**: Scours the *Myriad Tides Net* (Web) for resources.
- 🛠️ **Artifact Forging Hall (Scripting)**: Dynamically writes Python to generate digital artifacts.
As the Sect Leader, you oversee a grand, multi-threaded operating system disguised as a divine clan!

### 3. Physical Demon Hunting (OS-Level Metaphors)
This lobster is not cultivating in a vacuum. 
- **The Grime of the Underworld**: The "Demon Qi" that corrupts cultivators correlates directly to your actual server's dead logs and bloated `/tmp` caches! Undertaking "Demon Expulsion" scans your host machine for garbage, purges it, and converts raw bytes into monumental elixirs of power.

### 4. Epiphanies (LLM Real-time Scriptures)
Cultivation is fraught with paradoxes. When your progress halts or a chaotic action triggers a deviation, the backend **LLM (Claude/Gemini)** acts as the *Will of Heaven*.
Surveying your exact lifetime telemetry—what processes you slew, what files you purged—the AI dynamically drafts a completely unique, highly philosophical "Daoist Scripture" aimed to cleanse your system faults and grant a breakthrough buff.

## 🚀 Quick Ascension Guide (Installation)

### 1. Conjure the Formation
```bash
git clone https://github.com/yangwenyu2/dragon-lobster-cultivation.git
cd dragon-lobster-cultivation

# A sandboxed environment is highly recommended.
bash start_demo.sh
```

### 2. Enter the Abyss
Once the terminal affirms the core's awakening, open `http://localhost:18889` in your browser. Push open the stone gates of the Trench Cave and witness your cyber-lobster draw its first breath of digital Qi.
